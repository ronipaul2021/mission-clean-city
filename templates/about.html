About Birnagar

A century of service. A future of smart governance. We are building the cleanest, most connected municipality in West Bengal.

Our Mission

To make Birnagar the Number 1 Clean City in West Bengal by empowering every citizen to directly participate in civic governance. We believe transparency and technology together can transform how a municipality serves its people.

Our Vision

A Birnagar where no pothole goes unnoticed, no streetlight stays broken, and no citizen's voice goes unheard. A smart, responsive, and accountable municipality for every resident of all 14 wards.
